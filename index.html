Pst. Justice Brodrick

Pastor Justice Okeoghene Brodrick is a passionate servant of God with the Assemblies of God Church, Nigeria, dedicated to spreading the gospel and inspiring others to live Christ-centered lives. He helps both individuals and businesses discover their God-given purpose, offering spiritual guidance and practical insights for growth. With a Bachelor's degree in Economics and a Masterâ€™s in Petroleum Economics, Management, and Strategy, Pastor Brodrick combines his academic achievements with his expertise in data analysis, brand building, and business strategy. He works with small startups and large corporations, helping them grow while staying aligned with their core values.

Pastor Brodrick is also deeply committed to mentoring young people, guiding them to unlock their potential and fulfill their God-given purpose. He is happily married with three children, and his family is a cornerstone of his personal and ministry life.

Through his podcast, Pastor Brodrick shares powerful scriptural insights and practical wisdom, encouraging listeners to embrace their faith and find purpose in every aspect of life. Whether seeking spiritual guidance or business strategies, his teachings offer something for everyone, making a lasting impact on all who engage with his work.
